
**Project:** Gram Panchayat Dongargaon Railway Website

---

# 1. Project Overview

**Project Name:** Gram Panchayat Dongargaon Railway Digital Portal
**Location:** Dongargaon Railway, Warora Taluka, Chandrapur District, Maharashtra, India
**Village Count:** 1
**Population:** ~2000 (2001 Census)

The website will function as a **digital information portal and service platform** for the citizens of Dongargaon Railway. It will provide access to Panchayat information, online services, government schemes, transportation information, and public notices.

---

# 2. Objectives

* Provide a **central digital platform** for village information.
* Enable **online service requests and complaint management**.
* Improve **transparency of Panchayat operations**.
* Inform citizens about **government schemes and opportunities**.
* Provide **local transportation and nearby industry information**.
* Promote **nearby tourist places and village visibility**.
* **Ensure transparency in village development works via digital tracking.**
* **Strengthen Gram Sabha participation through digital records and calendars.**
* **Provide vital agricultural, employment, and emergency information to residents.**
* **Enable a mobile-friendly, low-bandwidth experience for rural accessibility.**

---

# 5.12 Village Transportation Information

### Railway Information

Nearest Railway Station:
**Chikni Road Railway Station**

Passenger train services:

1. **Wardha → Ballarshah Passenger**

   * Stops at Chikni Road
   * Approx time: **7:30 AM**

2. **Ballarshah → Wardha Passenger**

   * Stops at Chikni Road
   * Approx time: **8:00 PM**

---

### Bus Transport

Bus Route: **Warora – Kharwad**

* Buses pass through **Dongargaon (Railway)**.
* Frequency: **Every 45 minutes**

This information helps villagers and visitors plan travel.
